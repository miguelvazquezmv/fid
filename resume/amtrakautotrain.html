Amtrak Auto Train

Amtrak P052, the northbound Auto Train, crosses over the Lake Monroe drawbridge in Sanford, FL after its departure from the Auto Train terminal on their northward journey towards Lorton, VA. Taken in March 2025, this was marked as my last time seen the Auto Train led by a GE P40DC engine, as they are soon to be retired from service in favor of the Siemens ALC-42 engines, as seen in the trailing engine.
