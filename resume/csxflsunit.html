CSX 1972: Family Lines System Unit

Taken in Feburary 2025, southbound CSX manifest train M457 approaches the W Taft Vineland Rd crossing south of Orlando as it slowly reaching the Taft Yard. The leading locomotive of this train was a GE ES44AC engine No. 1972 in a hearitage unit, known as the Family Lines System unit, and its road number was named after one of CSX's predecessors was founded.
