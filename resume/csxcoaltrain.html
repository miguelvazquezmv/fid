CSX OUC Coal Train

CSX C040, a southbound loaded coal train from Indiana, slowly enters the OUC Stanton Energy Center, located in a few miles south of Avalon Park in Orlando. Seen in the rear of the train, a GE ES44AC-H engine No. 3243, serves as a push-pull engine for this coal train.
