Florida Passnger Rail System Map

Created from Adobe Illustrator, this Subway-styled map shows the routes where passnger trains operates around Florida, both existing and future services. A detailed look of the line also represents the color schemes of the rail operator the represents the route; For example, the orange line represents SunRail as it travels between Kissimmee and DeLand, and the yellow line represents Brightline, since it took inspirations from fleets and it currently travels between Orlando and Miami via Cocoa.
