CSX Office Car Special

CSX P001, the northbound Office Car Special (OCS) executive train, makes a very special appearence bypassing out the Orlando Amtrak station on its northward journey towards Jacksonville via the A-Line. Led by a trio of EMD F40PH engines, this train previously operated its southward journey towards Miami via Wildwood on the S-Line and was considered to be one of the rarest train that comes into Orlando.
